Recent Work

The Jacobian of a regular orthogonal matroid and torsor structures on spanning quasi-trees of ribbon graphs

With Matthew Baker and Changxin Ding. Adv. Math., September 2026.

We show that the set of spanning quasi-trees of a ribbon graph inherits a group structure from its Jacobian group without a distinguished identity element.

Baker-Bowler theory for Lagrangian Grassmannians

Int. Math. Res. Not. IMRN, April 2025.

Introduces antisymmetric matroids, a combinatorial object related to ribbon graphs, symplectic Grassmannians, delta-matroids, and gaussoids.

DOIarXiv

Orthogonal matroids over tracts

With Tong Jin. Forum Math. Sigma, August 2025.

Extends the theory of matroids with coefficients to orthogonal matroids, equivalently even delta-matroids.
